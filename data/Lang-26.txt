This resulted in incorrect formatted date strings for certain locales, as demonstrated by the `testLang645` test case, which failed to produce the expected output (`"fredag, week 53"`) for the Swedish locale (`sv_SE`). The evidence comes from the test case using the pattern `"EEEE', week 'ww"`, where the improper handling of the locale caused deviations in the week number. The issue was resolved by removing the `mLocale` parameter from the `GregorianCalendar` constructor, ensuring consistent behavior across locales and passing the test case.
