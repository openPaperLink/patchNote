The bug was caused by an incorrect loop-based calculation of `indices[last]` in the multidimensional counter, where the logic failed to handle certain cases, such as when `index` was smaller than the initial `count`. This resulted in incorrect mappings between unidimensional and multidimensional indices, breaking the consistency of the iteration logic and causing failures in tests like `testIterationConsistency`. Evidence for the issue came from this failing test, which highlighted mismatches between expected and actual multidimensional indices. The bug was resolved by replacing the loop with a direct assignment, `indices[last] = index - count`, ensuring accurate computation without iterative adjustments.
