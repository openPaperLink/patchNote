The bug was caused by the absence of proper HTML escaping for the `toolTipText` parameter, allowing special characters like quotes and ampersands to produce invalid or malformed HTML. This impacted tooltips by breaking the HTML structure, leading to rendering issues in web browsers and a poor user experience. Evidence for this issue is demonstrated in the `testGenerateURLFragment` unit test, where input such as `"Series \"A\", 100.0"` resulted in incorrect output due to unescaped double quotes conflicting with HTML syntax. The bug was resolved by applying the `ImageMapUtilities.htmlEscape` method to the `toolTipText`, ensuring special characters are converted to HTML entities and producing valid HTML output.
