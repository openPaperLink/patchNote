The bug in the `TimeSeries` class's `createCopy` method was caused by the failure to reset the `minY` and `maxY` values, resulting in these values being incorrectly carried over from the original series instead of being recalculated for the subset of data in the copy. This led to inaccurate `minY` and `maxY` values in the copied `TimeSeries` object, potentially causing incorrect behavior in calculations or visualizations. Evidence for this issue was observed in the `testCreateCopy3` unit test, where the copied series retained incorrect `minY` and `maxY` values from the original series. The bug was resolved by explicitly resetting `minY` and `maxY` to `Double.NaN` in the `createCopy` method, ensuring these values are recalculated for the copied subset.
