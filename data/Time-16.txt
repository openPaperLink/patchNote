The bug was caused by the incorrect use of the static `iDefaultYear` variable in the `DateTimeParserBucket` constructor, instead of dynamically determining the year based on `instantLocal` and the chronology's year field. This led to parsing errors where the resulting `MutableDateTime` object could have an incorrect year, causing unexpected behavior in date calculations and comparisons. Unit tests, such as `testParseInto_monthOnly` and `testParseInto_monthDay_withDefaultYear_feb29`, demonstrated the issue by failing when the expected year did not match the default year. The patch resolved the bug by replacing `iDefaultYear` with `chrono.year().get(instantLocal)`, ensuring the year is dynamically derived from the context of the provided instant.
