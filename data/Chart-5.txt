The bug in the `addOrUpdate` method of the `XYSeries` class is caused by a logical error where duplicate X-values are incorrectly checked even when the `allowDuplicateXValues` flag is set to `true`. This results in unintended overwriting of existing values instead of allowing duplicates, leading to incorrect data manipulation and an inconsistent series state. Evidence of the issue is demonstrated by the failing unit test `testBug1955483`, which expects two items with the same X-value to be added when duplicates are allowed but observes overwriting instead. The patch resolves the bug by introducing an early return when `allowDuplicateXValues` is `true,` bypassing the duplicate-checking logic and directly adding the new item to ensure correct handling of duplicate X-values.
