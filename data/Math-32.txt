The bug in the PolygonsSet class was caused by an incomplete condition without verifying if "tree.getCut()" was null, which means that current region was not cut into sub-hyperplanes. Only when the region has no boundary(v.length == 0), and the region is inside the polygon, and the region is not cut into sub-hyperplanes, the region covers whole space.  As a result, it sometimes misidentified regions as covering the entire space, causing errors in size and barycenter calculations and impacting downstream computations. Evidence for this issue was observed in the failing `testIssue780` unit test, which highlighted mismatches between expected and actual results due to the flawed condition. The bug was resolved by enhancing the condition to include a check for "tree.getCut() == null", ensuring accurate identification of the leaf BSPTree node .
