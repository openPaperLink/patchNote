Its impact was incorrect date calculations in edge cases, such as transitioning between months with different day counts or handling negative month adjustments, resulting in unexpected behavior during date manipulations. Evidence supporting this analysis includes failing unit tests like `testPlusMonths_int_negativeEndOfMonthAdjust` and `testMinusMonths_int_endOfMonthAdjust`, which highlighted issues with month adjustments around leap years and month boundaries. The bug was resolved by adding a conditional check to handle these cases, using modular arithmetic to calculate the correct month value and updating the `ReadablePartial` object accordingly.
